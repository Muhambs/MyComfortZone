Who We Are

"In addition to the comprehensive content we offer, My ComfortZone also provides an active and supportive community platform that allows every user to connect with others facing similar imaginations and experiences. It's a space where you can share experiences, receive support, and vent together with people who understand your situation. Therefore, we are committed to maintaining a proper and acceptable environment where everyone can feel comfortable to share and communicate.

Our Mission

Is to provide treatment and support in mental health to achieve happiness and satisfaction in their lives, allowing everyone access to help at any time and anywhere with high privacy and at the best prices, in addition to spreading awareness of the importance of mental health and its impact on the individual, family, and environment. We believe that your mental health is your true wealth, so we stand by your side on your journey to a healthy mind in a professional manner, without prejudice and in complete confidentiality.

Our Therapists

My ComfortZone provides psychiatrists and therapists, and all experts have a master's degree in psychotherapy and extensive experience in clinical psychotherapy both nationally and internationally. Our team operates according to the highest professional standards and ethics, prioritizing the needs of the client. The platform also includes a group of psychiatrists and experts specializing in the treatment of mental illnesses and various disorders, such as: depression, anxiety, obsessive-compulsive disorders, and phobias.
